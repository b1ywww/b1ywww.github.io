$$ 3.问a，b取什么值时，线性方程组 \begin{cases} x_{1} + 2x_{2} + 2x_{3} = 2 \\
x_{1} + 3x_{2} + x_{3} = 3 \\
x_{1} + x_{2} + ax_{3} = b \end{cases} 有唯一解;无解;有无穷多个解？在有无穷多个解时，求它的通解. $$

$$ \begin{align} 解:&线性方程组的系数矩阵D= \begin{bmatrix} 1&2&2\\
1&3&1\\
1&1&a \end{bmatrix} 当 \begin{vmatrix} D \end{vmatrix} =0时方程组无解或有无穷多个解。\\
&即:a=3\\ \\
&D_{x_{1}}= \begin{bmatrix} 2&2&2\\
3&3&1\\
b&1&a \end{bmatrix}, D_{x_{2}}= \begin{bmatrix} 1&2&2\\
1&3&1\\
1&b&a \end{bmatrix}, D_{x_{3}}= \begin{bmatrix} 1&2&2\\
1&3&3\\
1&1&b \end{bmatrix}\\ \\
&当\begin{vmatrix}D_{x_{1}}\end{vmatrix}= \begin{vmatrix}D_{x_{2}}\end{vmatrix}= \begin{vmatrix}D_{x_{3}}\end{vmatrix}=0 时,线性方程组有无穷多个解\\ \\
&即 b = 1; \\ \\
&将a = 3,b = 1 代入，可得其增广矩阵为 \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1&2&2&2\\
1&3&1&3\\
1&1&3&1 \end{array} \right] \\ \\
&化为行最简式为 \left[ \begin{array}{ccc|c} 1&2&2&2\\
0&1&-1&1\\
0&0&0&0 \end{array} \right]\\ \\
&令x_{3} = t;\\ \\
&则x_{1} = -4t,x_{2} = t+1\\ \\
&当\begin{vmatrix}D_{x_{1}}\end{vmatrix}= \begin{vmatrix}D_{x_{2}}\end{vmatrix}= \begin{vmatrix}D_{x_{3}}\end{vmatrix}\not=0 \\ \\
&即a=3,b\not= 1 时，方程组无解 当a\not= 3时 有唯一解 \end{align} \\ \\ \\ \\ \\ \\
$$
